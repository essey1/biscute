About us

Biscuté At Its Core

Biscuté is a licensed cookie company that started production in December 2021 in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, near Adisu Gebya. Biscuté operates from a commercial kitchen baking belaoved cookie with flavours like Vanilla, Ginger, Cinnamon, and Chocolate; with plans to introduce more exciting flavours in near future.


Biscuté takes pride in our attention to detail, ensuring that each cookie delivers a delightful experience for our beloved customers. We believe in the power of delicious cookies to create positive vibes and spread joy to all cookie lovers out there.

Why Choose Biscuté

Honestly Biscuté is on a mission to spread inspiration, joy, and poitive vibes through our delicious and delightful cookies. Biscuté wants to be more than just a cookie company; we want to be a catalyst good moods and happy connections between everyone.
